## STEP 10 — Beckham Law Note (Special Tax Regime — LIRNR)

**Beckham Law (Ley 19/2003) — Updated 2026 Rules:**

| Feature | Detail |
|---|---|
| Tax rate | Flat **24%** on Spanish-source income up to €600,000/year |
| Above €600,000 | 48% rate applies |
| Duration | **6 years total** (year of arrival + 5 additional years) |
| Application deadline | **Within 6 months** of registering with Spanish Social Security — **CRITICAL: missing this window = permanent loss of benefit** |
| Application form | **Form 149** filed with Spanish Tax Agency (AEAT) |
| Foreign income | Generally exempt from Spanish tax (subject to double-taxation treaty) |
| Pakistan-Spain treaty | ⚠️ Pakistan does NOT have a double taxation treaty with Spain — advise client accordingly |
| Below €30,000/year | Standard progressive IRPF may actually be lower than 24% flat rate — check both |
| Golden Visa | ❌ **Eliminated in 2025** — DNV is now the primary non-EU remote work route |

**Key 2026 Beckham Law Updates:**
- Must have qualifying employment/freelance contract to be eligible
- Apply Form 149 immediately after Social Security registration — do not wait
- Strongly recommend client engage a Spanish gestora (tax advisor) within first month of arrival

## RENEWAL GUIDANCE

Spain DNV granted initially for 1 year, renewable for 2-year periods up to 5 years total.
Renewal requires: proof of continued remote income, updated health insurance, clean record in Spain.
After 5 years: eligible to apply for long-term EU residence permit.
After 10 years: eligible for Spanish citizenship (naturalisation).
